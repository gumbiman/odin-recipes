Lasagna Recipe

This is lasanga and it's super tasty.

Ingredients:
  • 100 cups of cheddar cheese.
  • 10 dashes of red pepper.
  • 1 strand of witch's hair.
Steps:
  1. In a baking dish, add the cheese.
  2. sprinkle in the red pepper
  3. Cook on high at 595 degrees F for 99 minutes.
  4. Garnish with the witch's hair.
